Tech-U’s Dr. Kayode Adegoke Ranks Among the Top 2% of Scientists in the World

Abiola Ajimobi Technical University (Tech-U) celebrates a significant achievement as Dr. Kayode Adesina Adegoke, a scholar from the Department of Industrial Chemistry, has been ranked among the Top 2% of scientists in the world, as published on 16th September 2024 by Stanford University (USA) in collaboration with Elsevier B.V. (Netherlands). This global recognition reflects Tech-U’s growing academic influence and commitment to excellence in research.


A Global Honor

Dr. Adegoke featured as 48th in Nigeria and 1281st among 115,291 scholars worldwide in Environmental Sciences. This prestigious list identifies the world’s leading researchers across 22 academic fields and 174 sub-fields, marking Dr. Adegoke’s place among an elite group of scientists whose research significantly advances global knowledge.


The ranking is based on a meticulous analysis of bibliometric data from Scopus, the leading database for peer-reviewed literature. Metrics considered include the number of citations, author positions (single, first, last author), and co-authorship, alongside standardized data on h-index and i10 index. Dr. Adegoke’s inclusion in the Top 2% is a testament to his impactful research in the field of Environmental Sciences.


Research Contributions and Impact

Dr. Adegoke’s research interests span two major areas:

1. Energy Conversion: Focused on greenhouse gas conversion, such as turning carbon dioxide (CO₂) into value-added chemicals and fuels, along with hydrogen and oxygen production. His work also explores fuel cell applications like alcohol oxidation.

2. Wastewater Treatment: Dedicated to converting waste materials into adsorbents. His work has developed cost-effective alternatives to commercial activated carbon, which are used for removing pollutants in wastewater treatment plants.


As of September 2024, Dr. Adegoke has authored 96 articles, with 78 of these papers included in the 2024 Top 2% world ranking. His research enjoys 83.1% international collaboration and is published in top-tier journals, further establishing him as a respected figure in the global scientific community.


A Record of Excellence

Dr. Adegoke’s contributions to science extend beyond his research. His work has garnered over 4,243 citations on Google Scholar, with an h-index of 32 and an i10 index of 64, reflecting the lasting impact of his work in environmental sciences. These numbers highlight his increasing global visibility and his role as a young Nigerian scholar making significant contributions to solving critical environmental issues.


Tech-U's Growing Reputation

This remarkable achievement not only elevates Dr. Adegoke's standing but also places Abiola Ajimobi Technical University (Tech-U) among the Top 2% globally, highlighting the university’s dedication to advancing academic and scientific research. As Tech-U continues to support groundbreaking research and innovation, this global recognition reinforces the university’s commitment to producing globally competitive scholars.


Conclusion

Tech-U celebrates Dr. Kayode Adegoke’s global recognition and extends its congratulations for this remarkable achievement. His inclusion in the Top 2% of scientists worldwide is a source of pride for the entire university community. We remain committed to fostering academic excellence and supporting our scholars in making a global impact.
